> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud Journey program. This will help the FCJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The environment is friendly and open. Team members are always supportive. The comfortable workspace helped me focus.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentor provided detailed and dedicated guidance. The admin team was very helpful. I was encouraged to solve problems independently to learn better.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The tasks perfectly matched my academic background, reinforcing my foundational knowledge and building practical AWS skills.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
I gained hands-on AWS skills, teamwork, and professional communication. My mentor's advice helped clarify my career path.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The culture is positive and collaborative. Everyone is respectful, making me feel like a valued part of the team.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
Great support policies, flexible hours, and valuable internal training sessions.

---

### Additional Questions

- **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
  Working directly on real AWS cloud environments boosted my confidence significantly.

- **What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**  
  A brief orientation session at the very beginning to clarify the learning path would be helpful.

- **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**  
  Absolutely. It's a fantastic place to start learning AWS, thanks to great mentors and a supportive community.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations

- **Suggestions to improve the internship experience:**  
  More career sharing sessions from senior professionals would be highly appreciated.

- **Would you like to continue this program in the future?**  
  Having recently completed my university studies, my current plan is to dedicate my time to improving my English and pursuing professional certifications for a career in Software Testing. Nevertheless, the experience gained at FCJ will always be a valuable asset to me.

- **Any other comments:**  
  Thank you to the FCJ team and AWS Vietnam for providing me with such a meaningful internship opportunity.
